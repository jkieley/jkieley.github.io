Quick Pass

This app is meant to store all transportation passes such as your train ticket, bus pass, or subway pass all in one location. No longer will you have to struggle to find your pass or worry about getting it ruined. This application provides a convenient stress free way to manage all of your passes.

For email

Enter email used initially to set up account. The email should be in the form of noname@gmail.com .

For password

Enter password created when setting up account. The password should contain 6-12 characters and at least one capital letter.

For upload

To upload a new transportation pass click the plus button located in the wallet screen. Take picture of transportation pass so that it is clear and legible. After the desired picture has been captured, click upload and the pass will now be available for use.

Note: for this prototype the upload button is on default mode and will automatically upload a preset pass.

Wallet

Wallet displays the transportation passes that the user has uploaded. The user can click on individual passes to view a specific one or browse all of them at once. If a specific pass if clicked it will direct you to the QVC code.

Logout

Logout will direct you back to the sign in page.

Sign in

Enter in email and password. Once these have been verified, click the blue button labeled as sign in. Then proceed to the next pages and view passes or any desired information.

Sign up

If you do not have an account, click sign in. This button is in green. You will be prompted to enter an email and create a password. Once the fields have been met click sign in. You will then be directed to the main page and are able to upload transportation passes and view account information.

My profile

Click on my profile to view account details like email. Here you are able to change the password or simply close out of it.
